An exciting new mod

AvatarMod2 is a free community mod for Minecraft which will add elemental bending from the TV show Avatar: The Last Airbender. Players will be able to "bend" the elements of Minecraft and manipulate their environment to defeat enemies.

The mod will support four main types of bending styles, known as Airbending, Waterbending, Firebending, and Earthbending. Each bending style has its own strengths and strategies, requiring benders to take a unique role in combat. In addition, there are non-combat aspects to bending which will improve players' proficiency as they take on peaceful tasks in Minecraft.

PvP is a large part of the vision of this mod. Multiplayer will allow players to participate in combat and other interactions not normally present in singleplayer. This mod will introduce new strategy and challenge to existing PvP scenarios.

This mod is currently in Alpha but development is chugging along quite nicely and features are frequently added.

Quick Radial-Menu Ability System

AV2 brings a new system of ability activation to the table. Forget silly items or obscure keybindings for every ability: Manage up to 32 abilities with lightning-fast access via AV2's innovative Radial Menu system.

Fast-paced combat

With a diverse set of Abilities, combat in AV2 goes beyond the vanilla spam-clicking panic mechanic. Deal damage from afar with Thrown Blocks, evade your enemies with Airbending, or fight close-combat melees with Firebending.

Available now for Minecraft 1.11

Alpha release 2.0 is available for download now. Only MC 1.11 on PC is supported. Forge is required.

Installation instructions: install Forge and place the downloaded avatar mod JAR into your mods folder.
